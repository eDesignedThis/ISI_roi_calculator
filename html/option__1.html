ROI Calculator

Calculate the projected ROI for an incentive program.

Instructions
Steps for Using Incentive Solutions' ROI Calculator
  1. Enter the number of participants you plan on enrolling in your incentive program.
  2. Enter the average sales volume for each participant.
  3. Enter your average gross margin of sales.
  4. Enter the percent of sales that will be allocated to rewarding participants. Most businesses allocate between 1% and 2%, depending on average salves volume per participant and the desired value proposition.
  5. Enter the current ROI on your average marketing dollar. According to Nielson Global Media, the average ROI on each marketing dollar is 9%.
  6. Fill out the remaining fields for an in-depth ROI analysis and a PDF of your results to show your coworkers!

Additionally, there are strategies we can explore to increase your business growth and improve your distribution channel sales. The ROI calculator is intended to give you a ballpark estimate, but we encourage you to discuss possibilities for you company's incentive program with an ROI-certified expert.
